Skills

Empowering children and nurturing young minds with a range of skills to reach their full potential. Our services focus on developing essential skills in:

WIG/Hair Making

Hair-tastic adventures ahead! Join us in learning the exciting skill of wig and hair making, designed just for your kids

Robotics//Programming

Code, create, and innovate! Learn the exciting skills of robotics and programming, and bring your ideas to life

Dress Making

Unleash your inner fashion designer! Our dress making classes are designed to inspire young minds to create and express themselves through fashion

Creative Arts

Where creativity knows no bounds! Join us in the exciting journey of discovering art, expression, and self-expression.

Abacus

Count, calculate, and conquer! Our abacus classes are the perfect place for kids to develop their math skills and confidence.
